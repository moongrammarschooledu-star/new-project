About Me

A lifelong educator committed to academic excellence, discipline, and student development.

I am Muhammad Munawar, a mathematics educator with an M.Sc in Mathematics and 16 years of teaching experience across schools, academies, and one-on-one home tutoring. I have served as faculty at Divisional Public School and M.Q. Foundation High School, Model Town.

Over the years I have taught more than a hundred students who went on to remarkable careers — today they serve as doctors, engineers, and CSS officers working as Assistant Commissioners, while many others hold well-known positions in Pakistan and abroad.

My approach is simple: build strong concepts, keep students disciplined and motivated, and give each one the individual attention they deserve.
